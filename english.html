About Us
How is one supposed to know that the eelgrass in Skälderviken functions as a nursing ground for many marine species, and therefore is of great importance for the local fish populations?
How can one ever admire the beautiful sea anemone and the soft coral dead mans hand up close if one do not scuba dive?
How can one understand the relationship between a healthy marine environment, fish and shellfish on the food plate and our own modern lifestyle, if knowledge regarding the marine ecosystems and the footprints we humans leave behind is lacking?

At Mitt Hav we want to encourage curiosity, and increase the knowledge and understanding for the marine environment and the species found in Swedish waters.


Throughout the summer of 2016 we offer a temporary exhibition about the local marine environment, and aquariums with organisms from southern Kattegat, northern Öresund and Skälderviken. Especially exciting, for both young and old visitors, is of course our touch tank where you can hold a sea star in your hand and discover some of our marine life up close.

June - August
Crab Fishing Competitions

We arrange exciting crab fishing competitions during the whole summer. Everybody is welcome and there is no participation fee. No pre-registration is needed.

Crab Fishing Competitions June - August

Don’t forget to bring your own equipment and bait. Landing net is NOT allowed. Bring your friends and find out which one of you can catch the most and the largest crabs!

The winners from previous crab fishing competitions participate for free. Other participants pay 20 SEK. Nice prizes to the winners of the final. Maybe you will be the one to catch the most crabs, the largest or the smallest crab?

NOTE! Changes in time and location may occur. Visit our website for latest updates.
